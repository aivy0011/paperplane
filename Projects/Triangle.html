Triangle

This is based on the triangle assignment that was given to us in CSC116. You will create a program that draws a design made up of 1 - 5 rows of equilateral triangles. The length of each side of a triangle is 100 pixels. Each row will contain one more triangle than the row above it. The triangles are contiguous and the lower left/right points of each triangle form the uppermost points of the triangles in the row below. The (x, y) coordinates of the topmost point of the triangle in the first row must be (300, 50). The user will create a custom color for the design by specifying the amount of red, green, and blue used to draw it. The user will also specify the number of rows of triangles.
